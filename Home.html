We are 512 Hyperloop

Concept


In 2013, Elon Musk proposed a new mode of high-speed transport. He described a pod moving through an evacuated chamber, carrying people at speeds up to 760 mph. He envisioned the Hyperloop.

Our Mission


512 Hyperloop strives to design and build a scalable pod for the SpaceX Hyperloop Competition.

What We've Achieved


We passed the Preliinary Design Phase of the Hyperloop competition. We was not selected to move forward at Design Weekend, but we still building the Hyperloop pod and testing it on the Hawthorne Track.
